# Course Blueprint

This course is built as a practical lab, not as hype. You will start with a narrow, local-only setup, learn why that is the safest and sanest first move, then add just enough model selection, Docker knowledge, and security judgment to decide whether OpenClaw deserves a permanent place on your Mac.

## Modules
1. Understand the System | Learn what OpenClaw is, why people care about it, and why a self-hosted agent should be treated as a powerful tool rather than a toy.
2. Build the First Safe Lab | Prepare your Mac, choose a starter local model, and get a first dashboard-only OpenClaw setup running one step at a time.
3. Harden and Recover | Lock down the basic security posture, understand what Docker helps with and what it does not, and learn how to avoid common beginner mistakes.
4. Decide Whether It Earns a Spot | Tune model choices for your hardware, evaluate useful real-world workflows, and decide whether to keep, reset, or expand your setup.
